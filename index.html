App for Better Patient Communication

It is always a challenge for the doctor to communicate well with the patient. Often patients do not get enough information about their medical status. Also, when patient visits a new doctor, it may be quite hard for the doctor to find out about patient's medical history. The aim of our team is to develop intuitive solution for doctors and patients to communicate.
