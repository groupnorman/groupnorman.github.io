App for better patient communication

Challenge

Doctors tend to communicate with their patients behind the screens without having contact to them. The aim of this project is to improve communication between the doctor and the patient with technol- ogy, help the doctor with diagnosis and show patient info like previous visits, medication and visual- ize the injuries and issues of the patient with 3D-models.
